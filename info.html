Random Magic Items

Purpose

I wanted to create a way for dungeon masters to quickly and easily find magic items with which to reward their players, and to be able to do so with an element of randomness, if they so wish.

Credits

Thank you to Open5e for their api, from which I used their data to supplement my website. This would have been much more difficult without them.
